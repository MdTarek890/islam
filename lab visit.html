Lab Visit

Lab is shorthand for laboratory, with its Medieval Latin root laboratorium, "a place for labor or work," from the Latin laborare, "to work." Many scientists and researchers go to work each day in a lab (often wearing a "lab coat," a white smock that protects their clothes).

Laboratory sessions provide experiential learning sessions for students outside of the lecture hall. In labs, students work in small teams as they practice and develop procedural skills necessary to explore a phenomenon, conduct experiments, write code, collect data, design and test a prototype, etc. Laboratory classes provide students with first-hand experience with course concepts and with the opportunity to explore methods used by scientists in their discipline. Leading a laboratory session has particular challenges and opportunities that differ from those in a standard classroom environment.

实验室课程为讲堂外的学生提供体验式学习课程。在实验室中，学生以小组形式工作，练习和发展探索现象、进行实验、编写代码、收集数据、设计和测试原型等所需的程序技能。实验室课程为学生提供课程概念的第一手经验并有机会探索科学家在其学科中使用的方法。领导实验室课程面临着与标准课堂环境不同的特殊挑战和机遇。

A laboratory is a facility that provides controlled conditions in which scientific or technological research, experiments, and measurement may be performed. Laboratories are found in a variety of settings such as schools, universities, privately owned research institutions, corporate research and testing facilities, government regulatory and forensic investigation centers, physicians' offices, clinics, hospitals, regional and national referral centers, and even occasionally personal residences. Practical and laboratory-based sessions normally include a strong demonstration element, for example important techniques or how to use specific equipment, and they encourage students to have a go themselves.

实验室是提供可进行科学或技术研究、实验和测量的受控条件的设施。实验室存在于各种环境中，例如学校、大学、私营研究机构、企业研究和测试设施、政府监管和法医调查中心、医生办公室、诊所、医院、地区和国家转诊中心，甚至偶尔还有个人实验室。住宅。实践和基于实验室的课程通常包括强大的演示元素，例如重要的技术或如何使用特定设备，并且鼓励学生自己尝试。
