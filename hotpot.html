Hotpot

The Chinese name for hotpot, Huo Guo/火锅, considered a main course and is usually served without rice or noodles on the side. Hot pots can be prepared and eaten at home or in a restaurant. Typical hot pot ingredients include thinly sliced meat, leaf vegetables, mushrooms, vermicelli, sliced potatoes, bean products, egg dumplings, tofu, and seafood

Hotpot is comforting, joyful, delicious.Hotpot is also an interactive dining style that lets you assemble your own collection of fresh ingredients to cook at the table in a simmering pot of flavorful soup.Choose from our selection of raw meats, seafoods, vegetables, tofus, noodles, and more to cook at your own pace.Hot pot is a flavorful broth traditionally served inside a large metal pot. The broth is brought to a boil and left simmering for the duration of the meal. Raw ingredients, such as meat and vegetables, are placed into the simmering broth and thus cooked. The cooked pieces are dipped into dipping sauces for additional flavor. Hot pot is considered a main course and is usually served without rice or noodles on the side. Hot pots can be prepared and eaten at home or in a restaurant.

火锅是舒适、快乐、美味的。火锅也是一种互动的用餐方式，让您可以在餐桌上组装自己收集的新鲜食材，用慢炖锅煮出美味的汤。从我们精选的生肉、海鲜、蔬菜、豆腐、面条等，您可以按照自己的节奏烹饪。火锅是一种传统上在大金属锅中盛放的美味肉汤。将肉汤煮沸，并在用餐期间小火慢煮。将肉类和蔬菜等原料放入沸腾的肉汤中煮熟。将煮熟的块浸入蘸酱中以增加风味。火锅被认为是主菜，通常不配米饭或面条。火锅可以在家或在餐馆准备和食用。

Hotpot ingredients include thinly sliced meat, leaf vegetables, mushrooms, vermicelli, sliced potatoes, bean products, egg dumplings, tofu, and seafood. Raw ingredients are pre-sliced into thin sections that will cook quickly and consistently in the simmering broth, which is kept at a gentle boiling temperature. Most raw foods can be cooked in a hot pot, although they may have different cooking times, and must be immersed in the soup and then removed accordingly.At the conclusion of the meal, the broth would have acquired many flavors from the added ingredients and may be served to the diners after turning off the heat source.

火锅配料包括肉片、叶类蔬菜、蘑菇、粉条、土豆片、豆制品、蛋饺、豆腐和海鲜。原料被预先切成薄片，在保持温和沸腾温度的小火汤中快速、一致地煮熟。大多数生食都可以在火锅中烹饪，尽管它们的烹饪时间可能不同，并且必须将其浸入汤中然后相应地取出。用餐结束时，肉汤会因添加的配料而获得许多风味，关掉热源后即可端给食客。
