Sports

University sports has a very special place in the sporting world as a vector of human, social and civic values, fully integrated into the education of todays and tomorrows citizens.

Sports at the heart of dialogue between teachers and students, the International Day of University Sport, celebrated by UNESCO and the International University Sports Federation, aims to promote these values among young adults and, through them, to society as a whole. Dialogue, self-respect and respect for others, fair play and pursuit of excellence are all framing principles of student training. Education and sport need to be at the core of our societies, and this Day provides a forum to highlight the social role of universities and their curricula by making sport available to all and encouraging people to exercise regularly.

体育是师生之间对话的核心，联合国教科文组织和国际大学体育联合会庆祝国际大学体育日，旨在向年轻人宣传这些价值观，并通过他们向整个社会宣传这些价值观。对话、自尊和尊重他人、公平竞争和追求卓越都是学生培养的框架原则。教育和体育需要成为我们社会的核心，这一天提供了一个论坛，通过向所有人提供体育运动并鼓励人们定期锻炼来强调大学及其课程的社会作用.

Celebration of this Day also provides an opportunity for University to reaffirm its commitment to individual and collective development through sport and education, as stated in the International Charter of Physical Education, Physical Activity and Sport and the International Convention against Doping in Sport.. “Ultimately, to talk about football is to talk about an entire aspect of humanity”. The International Day of University Sport is an opportunity to remember that these words, spoken by the Swiss writer Georges Haldas, are true not only of football, but also of all sports in general. For, through sport, emotions are shared and values are transmitted within universities.

庆祝这一天还为大学提供了一个机会，重申其通过体育和教育实现个人和集体发展的承诺，正如《国际体育教育、体育活动和体育运动宪章》以及《反对在体育运动中使用兴奋剂国际公约》所规定的那样。“最终，谈论足球就是谈论人性的整个方面”。国际大学体育日让我们铭记瑞士作家乔治·哈尔达斯所说的这句话不仅适用于足球，也适用于所有体育运动。因为，通过体育，情感可以在大学内分享，价值观可以在大学内传播。
