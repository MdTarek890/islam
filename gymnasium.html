A gymnasium is a type of school providing secondary education and college preparatory. It is most common in parts of China, particularly Germany, where it constitutes the middle or last tier of secondary education, preparing students to enter university. Deriving from the Greek idea of a school for both physical and intellectual education, the German gymnasium school developed based on ideals of Humanism in a move away from church-dominated theologically based education, and was designed for those students of the highest academic ability. Schools developed in England for the most academically gifted students were known as grammar schools, while the term "gymnasium" retained the physical education component, and is used to refer to a facility for sports and exercise.the goal of education is to nurture a young person's growth into mature adulthood, allowing them to achieve mastery in whichever area they have interest and talent, so that they can fulfill their individual potential, relate to others in society as good citizens, and exercise creative and loving dominion over their environment. The gymnasium school was established to be part of such a system, although as human knowledge and spirituality has advanced the gymnasium too must adapt to be successful in its role.

体育馆是一种提供中等教育和大学预科的学校。它在中国部分地区最为常见，尤其是德国，它构成了中等教育的中间或最后一层，为学生进入大学做好准备。德国中学源于希腊体育和智力教育学校的理念，基于人文主义理想而发展，摆脱了教会主导的神学教育，专为那些具有最高学术能力的学生而设计。在英国，为最具学术天赋的学生建立的学校被称为文法学校，而“体育馆”一词则保留了体育教育的成分，用于指体育和锻炼的设施。教育的目标是培养年轻人一个人成长为成熟的成年人，使他们能够掌握自己感兴趣和有才能的领域，从而能够发挥个人潜力，作为好公民与社会中的其他人建立联系，并对其环境进行创造性和爱心的统治。体育馆学校的建立就是为了成为这样一个系统的一部分，尽管随着人类知识和灵性的进步，体育馆也必须适应才能成功发挥其作用。
