Football

Football is a family of team sports that involve, to varying degrees, kicking a ball to score a goal. Unqualified, the word football normally means the form of football that is the most popular where the word is used. Sports commonly called football include association football.

Football, also called association football or soccer, is a game involving two teams of 11 players who try to maneuver the ball into the other team’s goal without using their hands or arms. The team that scores more goals wins. Football is the world’s most popular ball game in numbers of participants and spectators. Modern football originated in Britain in the 19th century. Though “folk football” had been played since medieval times with varying rules, the game began to be standardized when it was taken up as a winter game at public schools. The Football Association, formed in 1863, codified the rules of the game and hosted the first cup competition between regional football clubs in Britain.

足球，也称为英式橄榄球或英式足球，是一项由 11 名球员组成的两队比赛他们试图在不使用双手或手臂的情况下将球射入对方球门。进球多的球队获胜。就参与者和观众数量而言足球是世界上最受欢迎的球类运动。现代足球起源于19世纪的英国。尽管“民间足球”自中世纪以来就已存在规则各异但当它被作为公立学校的冬季比赛时这项运动就开始标准化。 1863年成立的足球协会制定了比赛规则并主办了英国首届地区足球俱乐部之间的杯赛。

Internationally, association football is governed by FIFA. Under FIFA, there are six continental confederations: AFC, CAF, CONCACAF, CONMEBOL, OFC, and UEFA. Of these confederations, CONMEBOL is the oldest one, being founded in 1916. National associations are responsible for managing the game in their own countries both professionally and at an amateur level, and coordinating competitions in accordance with the Laws of the Game.The most senior and prestigious international competitions are the FIFA World Cup and the FIFA Women's World Cup. The men's World Cup is the most-viewed sporting event in the world, surpassing the Olympic Games.

在国际上，足球协会由国际足联管辖。国际足联下辖六个洲足联：亚足联、非洲足联、中北美洲及加勒比海足联、南美足联、大洋洲足联和欧足联。在这些联合会中，CONMEBOL 是最古老的一个，成立于 1916 年。国家协会负责管理本国的职业和业余水平的比赛，并根据比赛规则协调比赛。著名的国际赛事有国际足联世界杯和国际足联女子世界杯。男子世界杯超越奥运会，成为全球收视率最高的体育赛事.
