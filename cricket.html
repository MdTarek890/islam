Cricket

Cricket is played with a bat and ball and involves two competing sides (teams) of 11 players. The field is oval with a rectangular area in the middle, known as the pitch, that is 22 yards (20.12 metres) by 10 feet (3.04 metres) wide. Two sets of three sticks, called wickets, are set in the ground at each end of the pitch.

Across the top of each wicket lie horizontal pieces called bails. The sides take turns at batting and bowling (pitching); each turn is called an “innings” (always plural). Sides have one or two innings each, depending on the prearranged duration of the match, the object being to score the most runs. The bowlers, delivering the ball with a straight arm, try to break (hit) the wicket with the ball so that the bails fall.his is one of several ways that the batsman is dismissed, or put out. A bowler delivers six balls at one wicket (thus completing an “over”), then a different player from his side bowls six balls to the opposite wicket. The batting side defends its wicket.

每个三柱门的顶部都有水平的部件，称为栏杆。双方轮流击球和投球（投球）；每回合称为“局”（总是复数）。双方各有一局或两局，具体取决于预先安排的比赛持续时间，目标是得分最多。投球手用直臂传球，尝试用球打破（击中）三柱门，使球落下。这是击球手被解雇或出局的几种方式之一。一名投球手向一个三柱门投出六个球（从而完成“结束”），然后本方的另一名球员向对面的三柱门投出六个球。击球方防守其三柱门。

There are two batsman up at a time, and the batsman being bowled to (the striker) tries to hit the ball away from the wicket. A hit may be defensive or offensive. A defensive hit may protect the wicket but leave the batsmen no time to run to the opposite wicket. In that case the batsmen need not run, and play will resume with another bowl. If the batsman can make an offensive hit, he and the second batsman (the nonstriker) at the other wicket change places. Each time both batsmen can reach the opposite wicket, one run is scored. Providing they have enough time without being caught out and dismissed, the batsmen may continue to cross back and forth between the wickets, earning an additional run for each time both reach the opposite side.

一次有两名击球手上场，被击球的击球手（前锋）试图将球击出三柱门。击中可能是防御性的，也可能是进攻性的。防守击球可能会保护三柱门，但使击球手没有时间跑到对面的三柱门。在这种情况下，击球手无需奔跑，比赛将继续进行另一个投球。如果击球手可以进攻击球，他和另一个三柱门的第二击球手（非击球手）交换位置。每次两名击球手都能到达对面的三柱门，就得分一分。如果击球手有足够的时间而不会被发现并被解雇，则击球手可以继续在三柱门之间来回穿越，每次到达对方时都会获得额外的分数.
